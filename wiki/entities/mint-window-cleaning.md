# Mint Window Cleaning

## Overview

Multi-market home service company focused on window cleaning. Part of a three-pillar ops transformation: bulletproof HubSpot/HCP, coaching/holding reps accountable, and automating 80% of low-leverage work.

## Business Context

**Current focus areas:**
- Revenue operations, sales, customer service, ops, reporting
- AI initiatives and automation
- Multi-market expansion

**Core software stack:**
- HubSpot (CRM, sales pipeline, deals)
- Housecall Pro (jobs, customers, estimates)
- Aircall (call logs, rep activity)
- Connecteam (employee scheduling/accountability)
- ClickUp (project management)

## Mission

*"Build and maintain a bulletproof operational system — optimizing every tool, workflow, and process for maximum sales conversion and customer retention — while coaching and holding human reps accountable, and systematically automating low-leverage work until a small, elite team runs the whole operation at scale."*
